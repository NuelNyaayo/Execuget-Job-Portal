Head Chef

KFC-Westlands.
  • Nairobi, Kenya
  • +25416741257

$4850 /monthly

Description

KFC Westlands is excited to announce the recruitment of a head chef. This is a fantastic opportunity for an experienced culinary expert to join a renowned team. The selected head chef will be offered a competitive salary of $4850 per month. This full-time position requires preparing a wide variety of cuisines, including Chinese, seafood, Indian food, and more. The ideal candidate will have a passion for cooking, exceptional culinary skills, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

In addition to a competitive salary, KFC Westlands offers a dynamic and supportive work environment where the head chef can thrive. The position includes benefits such as health insurance, opportunities for professional growth, and a chance to work with a dedicated team of culinary professionals. The head chef will be responsible for menu planning, ensuring food safety standards, and managing kitchen operations efficiently. This role is perfect for someone who is innovative, detail-oriented, and ready to take on a leadership role in a busy and popular restaurant.

Responsibilities

  • Lead and manage the kitchen staff, providing training, supervision, and motivation to ensure efficient kitchen operations.
  • Design and develop diverse menus that include Chinese, seafood, and Indian cuisines, ensuring variety and quality.
  • Oversee and maintain high standards of food safety and hygiene in the kitchen, ensuring compliance with health regulations.
  • Manage inventory, order supplies, and control food costs to maintain budget efficiency while minimizing waste.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a head chef or similar role.
  • Expertise in Chinese, seafood, and Indian cuisines.
  • Ability to manage and supervise kitchen staff effectively.
  • Strong knowledge of food safety and sanitation standards.
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills.
  • Creative in menu planning and presentation.
  • Ability to work under pressure in a fast-paced environment.
  • Hard worker and passionate & strong leadership and communication skills.

Educational Requirements

The head chef at KFC Westlands should have a degree or diploma in culinary arts or a related field. Extensive experience in diverse cuisines, especially Chinese, seafood, and Indian food, is essential. Additional certifications in food safety are highly desirable.

Working Hours

  • Weekly: 5 days.
  • from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM
  • Weekend: Saturday, Sunday.
  • from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • 40-50 hours per week

Benefits

  • Earn a lucrative salary of $4850 per month.
  • Comprehensive health coverage for peace of mind.
  • Opportunities for continuous learning and career advancement.
  • Enjoy vacation days and personal leave.
  • Discounts on meals and products at KFC.
  • Access to a retirement savings plan with employer contributions.
  • Performance-based bonuses and incentives.
  • Flexible scheduling options to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
  • Access to training and development programs to enhance culinary skills.
  • Freedom to innovate and create new menu items.
  • Work in a dynamic and supportive team environment.
  • Lead and manage a team of culinary professionals.
  • Gain experience working for a globally recognized brand.
  • Exposure to a variety of cuisines including Chinese, seafood, and Indian.
  • Secure and stable employment in a well-established company.
